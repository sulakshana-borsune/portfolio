Portfolio

Budget Tesla is a app which provides information about the charging stations available within a 60 miles radius for Tesla drivers. When we select the perticular charging station it will give us information about the address, phone number and the working hours.

This app also provides the weather information of any city under search. It gives us 5 day weather forecast which includes current temperature, maximum temperature, humidity, and the wind speed.
